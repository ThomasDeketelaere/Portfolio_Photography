About Me

Hey! I’m Thomas, 24, a wedding photographer based in Antwerp. I started taking photos simply because I loved capturing moments that tell a story, and somewhere along the way it became my mission to celebrate every love story in all its magic and uniqueness.

Every wedding is different, and I love capturing the little sparks, quiet glances, and big, messy moments that make yours unforgettable. I specialize in diversity (neurodivergence, LGBTQ+ couples, and beyond) and I welcome anyone and everyone to be celebrated through my lens.

When I’m not behind the camera, you’ll find me at my PC, exploring new corners of the world, or curled up with my cat, Nala.



I’m always up for a chat, so if you have any questions or just want to say hi, feel free to reach out!
You can find me on wedding Instagram profile or on my main profile.


Thanks for stopping by, and I can’t wait to capture your story!
